Users don’t want linear interfaces that make conclusions for them. They want access to tiered interfaces that allow them to personalize and customize the way they consume and report data.

Users expect a higher level of experience, interactivity, flexibility and accessibility – to information, collaborators and across devices.

“…Millennial’s are driving new expectations of work and business software. They approach software through a consumer lens; they expect simplicity and instant results.”

Users will have far greater flexibility in terms of where, how and when they access their information and connections.
