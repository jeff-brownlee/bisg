Millennials are the first generation to be bombarded with social networks and collaboration from early childhood.  They are the:

  • Google Generation: “I don’t need a library, I have Google’s search box”
  • First Digital Generation: “First Generation to be born into a fully digital world”
  • Generation We: “Inclusivity & Multiculturalism”

Their tolerance for poorly implemented information design is very low. They expect a higher level of complexity as a by-product of greater power and control over results. Millennials may be the early trend setters but all users are now expecting more. Users:

  • Receive information quickly from multiple multimedia sources
  • Process pictures, sounds, color and video before text
  • Prefer instant gratification and sophisticated understanding of delayed rewards
  • Expect immediate access to hyperlinked multimedia information
  • Network simultaneously with many others of various back- grounds and locations

Their world is fully connected, social & collaborative.  The tools will eventually match their thinking.
