It is no secret that Millennials are the generation on the go. Mobile phone, tablet computers, and Wi-Fi are technologies used every day, enabling Millennials to take their networks, friends and data with them. With 95% of Millennials online, it’s safe to say being connected is a daily part of their lives.

  • Hyper Connected: Nearly 6 in 10 American adults access the internet wirelessly through smartphones and computers. Millennials hold the top spot with 82 percent using wireless internet.
  • Gearing Up: Millennials are adopters of all the multitude of devices available to stay connected. 95% own a cell phone, 74% own an MP3 player, 63% own a game console, and 70% owning a laptop.
  • Overly Occupied: The activity most Millennials engage in online is sending and receiving e-mails, with 96 percent doing so online. They use their phone to take photos, play games, text message, shoot video, access the internet, e-mail, listen to music and instant message.
