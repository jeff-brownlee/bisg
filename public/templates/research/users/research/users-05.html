Millennial preferences will drive your enterprise evolution.  Don’t resist – engage and innovate.

  • Plan out your Multi-Device Support
    • Explore new technologies and approaches such as Responsive design to allow for users to access your BI Portal on the desktop as well as smartphones, tablets and smart TVs.
  • Engage your Millennials in Gamification Strategies
    • Millennials prefer to engage in non-linear ways.  Gamification is a great way to take the timeless aspects of ‘play’ and incorporate them into serious corporate efforts.
  • Integrate Learning & Training into your Portal
    • By incorporating interactive training tools into your software releases, Millennials learn as they ‘do’, as opposed to just a classroom or video.
