User preferences will drive your social evolution.  Don’t resist – engage and innovate.

  • Plan out your Multi-Device Support
    • Explore new technologies and approaches such as Responsive design to allow for users to access your content on the desktop as well as smartphones, tablets and smart TVs.
  • Engage users in Gamification Strategies
    • Millennials prefer to engage in non-linear ways.  Gamification is a great way to take the timeless aspects of ‘play’ and incorporate them into your user experiences.
  • Integrate User Generated Content & Peer Support
    • Integrating user generated content increases credibility and site traffic. Peer support can lower support costs and provide product fans a forum to advocate products.
