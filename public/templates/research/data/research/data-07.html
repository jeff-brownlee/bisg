Social BI allows for full data sharing not just as a forward point but in real-time whiteboard sharing and group collaboration with voice/video. The creation of reports occurs as a group identity. Across the organization, employees can collaborate, share and discuss BI data, from origin to publication, making the organization mind much smarter.

For business users, it will be very important to be able to access the right portion of the data at the right time. For example, when one is on the elevator with a prospective client, you’ll want a mobile C-Level highlight view with gesturing capabilities. Understanding the business user’s journey will help define how to optimize the view for each device setting.
