How users choose to interact with BI portal data is very limited today.Â  In the future, users will be able to choose the method of interaction that most fits the task at hand, the intent of use and their preferences. Options will include gesture-based controls, voice, and eye-tracking.

We’re seeing initial fragmentation in regards to interaction tools. Many of them seem very promising, but what’s missing is a practical approach to tools. What tool, what interface element is best-suited for the kind of data the user is interacting with? What is the user’s intent in regards to this interaction? In a decade, when the infatuation with the gadgetry is over, these questions will drive the technology winners.
