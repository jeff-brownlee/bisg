Future BI portals will have layers of interaction to enhance analytics for direct communication with visual data. Current BI Portal software provides a very limited approach to direct interaction with visuals. Future tools will allow for direct interaction with data visualizations.

Tiered views will anticipate user needs, allowing you to choose the depth of view based on your need whether it be glancing through “big picture” data or mining data story variables. No simple task, as architects and strategists must first define what variables should be exhibited at each tier, and in which manner. Before you can go in and choose your own data points to create a compelling data story, the structure and parameters must first be drawn out.

A consistent use of symmetry, iconography and color creates a visual language that guides the user more quickly from novice to expert. Often times, this visual language is an after-thought: color is based on the brand parent, stock icons are purchased and symmetry choices are left to the limitations of the CMS/Data engine.
