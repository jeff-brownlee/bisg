Gaming is a lateral industry where data visualization and information design is critically important. The information models are very mature. Gaming companies are no longer calling it an interface, but a HUD or heads up display. HUDs allow for a high level of customization and user-tweaking, and cottage industries have grown out of providing gamers with data visualization add-ons that fit a user’s particular needs.

Macro/Scripting Access involves creating a layer of customization enabling the power user to create his/her own modules. Creating a scripting/add-on layer for users is an innovation we see trending in the gaming world. It’s a powerful concept not yet fully explored in the BI Portal world.
