BISG Road Map

BISG has outlined an aggressive but reasonable road map for addressing the business requirements in a phased approach.

Phase 1: This initial deployment will be built in IBM Connections, primarily focused on core social functionality. This site will be publicly available but will only be promoted with a small number of internal users to gain feedback and begin building momentum for the larger deployment.

Phase 2: This deployment will be a full solution leveraging both IBM Connections and WebSphere Portal solutions. This broader deployment will provide a much richer set of functionality, more personalized marketing, and a richer user experience.
