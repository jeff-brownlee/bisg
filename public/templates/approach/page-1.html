UX Design Methodology

User experience is the quality of experience an individual has when interacting with a particular design, process or system.

  • Research & Strategy: Observational methods are used to identify and understand both the users’ and organization’s goals/tasks, pain points, and business requirements.
  • Modeling: Results of the Strategy and Research efforts are reviewed for common patterns that can be represented through a variety of formats including Personas and Scenarios.
  • Concept & Design: The requirements and models are analyzed to quickly and iteratively create wireframes and low fidelity prototypes to demonstrate requirements and interaction flows.
  • Evaluation: User-based assessments are formally carried out assessing whether components of the process, product or system meet end user requirements.
