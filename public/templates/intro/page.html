Canon BISG: UX and Technical Design

Hitachi Consulting is pleased to present our proposal for assisting Canon with UX and Technical Design for Phase 2 of the Production Printing Community portal.

Proposal Contents

Objective: uncover the future of business intelligence and its impact on the enterprise

This proposal contains for the following information:

  • Research – Supporting research that includes best practices for community interaction design and competitive analysis
  • Overview – Brief overview of Hitachi Consulting
  • Understanding – Our understanding of your needs for this initiative
  • Approach – A description of our methodology, approach, activities, and deliverables for this engagement
  • Examples – Initial creative comps to present a potential vision for the Production Printing proposal as well as case studies from similar projects
  • Proposal – A breakdown of proposed timeline, staffing, and fees for this engagement
  • Conclusion – Summary and contact information
